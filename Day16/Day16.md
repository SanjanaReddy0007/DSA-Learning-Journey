## 📖 What I Learned

- Sorting is often the first optimization step before solving complex problems.
- Many interview problems become easier after arranging data in ascending or descending order.
- Custom comparators help sort objects or pairs according to multiple conditions.
- Sorting helps eliminate unnecessary nested loops and improves efficiency.
- Greedy algorithms frequently depend on sorted input.
- Ranking and scheduling problems usually require sorting before processing.
- Interval-based problems become easier after sorting by starting or ending points.
- Sorting enables efficient searching using binary search techniques.
- Understanding time complexity helps in selecting the best sorting strategy.
- Practicing sorting-based problems improves pattern recognition for coding interviews.
