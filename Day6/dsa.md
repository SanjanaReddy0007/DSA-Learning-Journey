### 5. Factorial of a Number

Formula:

n! = n × (n-1)!

Example:

5! = 120

---

### 6. Double Factorial

Definition:

n!! = n × (n-2) × (n-4) ...

Example:

8!! = 8 × 6 × 4 × 2
