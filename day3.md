## Key Learnings

### GCD

The Greatest Common Divisor (GCD) is the largest number that divides two numbers without leaving a remainder.

Example:
GCD(12,18) = 6

### LCM

The Least Common Multiple (LCM) is the smallest number that is divisible by both numbers.

Example:
LCM(12,18) = 36

### GCD of Array

Finding the common divisor among all elements of an array.

### LCM of Array

Finding the common multiple among all elements of an array.
